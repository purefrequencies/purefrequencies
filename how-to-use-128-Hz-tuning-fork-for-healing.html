How to Use Tuning Forks for Healing

Step 1: Activate the Tuning Fork

Hold the stem firmly and gently strike the prongs against a rubber activator or the palm of your hand. Avoid hard surfaces, which can damage the fork or create harsh overtones.

Step 2: Apply to the Body

For a 128 Hz weighted tuning fork, place the base of the stem on areas like the sternum, shoulders, lower back, or the bottoms of the feet. These areas transmit vibration effectively through bone and tissue.

Step 3: Use Around the Energy Field

You can also move the activated fork slowly around the body without touching the skin. Many practitioners sweep it around the head and torso to encourage relaxation and energetic balance.

Step 4: Breathe and Relax

Pair the sound with slow, deep breathing. Sessions typically last 5–15 minutes. The goal is to let the nervous system settle while the vibration gently supports circulation and relaxation.
