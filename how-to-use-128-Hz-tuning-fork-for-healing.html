How to Use Tuning Forks for Healing

Step 1: Activate the Fork

Hold the stem and gently strike the prongs against a rubber activator or your palm. This creates a clean, steady vibration.

Step 2: Apply to the Body

Place the base of a weighted fork on areas like the chest, shoulders, spine, or feet. These areas help conduct vibration through the body.

Step 3: Use in the Energy Field

You can also hold the fork a few inches away from the body and move it slowly around areas that feel tense. Many people use this method around the head and heart space for calming effects.

Step 4: Breathe Slowly

Take deep, slow breaths while listening to the tone fade. A session can last 5–15 minutes and works best when you are relaxed and present.
